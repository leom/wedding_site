The HAC

image source

Just north of the City of London and near to Old Street and Moorgate stations, the the HAC has been the home of the Honourable Artillery Company since 1735 and is unlike any other place in London; a magnificent Georgian house set in a six acre garden located just a couple of minutes walk from Moorgate.

The HAC strongly encourages the use of public transport in order to minimise the environmental impact of events. You should note that we are in the London congestion zone http://www.tfl.gov.uk/roadusers/congestioncharging/ Cycle hire Barclays cycle hire is an excellent initiative for getting around London quickly. Cycles can be collected and returned at any of the cycle hire sites in London. The closest sites to the HAC are at Finsbury Square and Old Street both of which are a 2 minute walk away. For more information visit www.tfl.gov.uk/roadusers/cycling/15150.aspx Plan your journey at www.cyclejourneyplanner.tfl.gov.uk Closest tube Stations: Moorgate (walk north up City Rd) or Old Street (Exit 5 - walk south down City Rd) Both tube stations are a 3-4 minute walk to the HAC Closest main Line Stations: Liverpool Street (approximately a 10 minute walk) Moorgate (approx 5 minute walk) Our property frontage on City Road has a “castle” appearance and is situated directly next to 32-36 City Road and opposite the new Travelodge. Buses A number of bus routes operate along City Road. To help plan your journey on public transport click here to visit the Transport for London website Car parks: If you really do need to drive there is an NCP carpark at Finsbury Square almost opposite the HAC. Please note that we do not provide parking at the HAC other than for pre booked disabled badge holders.
