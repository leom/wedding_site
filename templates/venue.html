The HAC

image source

Just north of the City of London and near to Old Street and Moorgate stations, the the HAC has been the home of the Honourable Artillery Company since 1735 and is unlike any other place in London; a magnificent Georgian house set in a six acre garden located just a couple of minutes walk from Moorgate.

Closest tube stations
Moorgate (walk north up City Rd) or Old Street (Exit 5 - walk south down City Rd) Both tube stations are a 3-4 minute walk to the HAC

Free parking on the roads after 1pm for everyone (drivers please note, the HAC is in a London congestion zone).
